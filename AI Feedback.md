# Los Angeles & Santa Barbara — May 19–26, 2026

Two people, one week. Flying in from Tel Aviv (El Al). 

---

## Key Changes and Reasoning

* **Venice Relocation (Day 8 to Day 2):** Driving from Downtown LA to Venice on a Tuesday morning involves severe commuter traffic. Attempting to visit Gjusta and Abbot Kinney before a 1:30 PM flight from LAX would be stressful and risky. Venice is now on Day 2 while you are based nearby in Santa Monica.
* **Tourist Staples Added (Day 3):** The Hollywood Walk of Fame and TCL Chinese Theatre were added to your Thursday morning. While busy, they are essential for a classic "Tourist Mode" first visit.
* **Griffith Observatory Timing (Day 3):** Your arrival was shifted from 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM. Sunset in late May is around 7:55 PM; arriving at 5:30 PM leaves you waiting too long for the daylight-to-night transition.
* **Skid Row Avoidance (Day 7):** The planned walk from Grand Central Market to the Arts District on 6th Street was removed. This route goes directly through Skid Row, which is unsafe and highly unpleasant. You must take an Uber between these locations.

Four days in Los Angeles (Westside), a drive up the Pacific Coast Highway on Saturday, two nights in Santa Barbara, then back to LA (Downtown/Arts District) for the final stretch.

**Hotels**

## Day 1 — Tuesday, May 19 · Arrival & Santa Monica
- **2:55 PM:** LAX arrival — LY 5. Uber to hotel.
- **5:00 PM:** Hotel check-in, Santa Monica.
- **Evening:** Walk the Santa Monica Pier and beach path before sunset. Dinner on Main Street or Ocean Ave.

## Day 2 — Wednesday, May 20 · Venice & The Getty
- **8:30 AM:** Uber to Venice. Breakfast at Gjusta (320 Sunset Ave).
- **10:00 AM:** Stroll Abbot Kinney Boulevard and the Venice Canals.
- **1:00 PM:** Lunch in Brentwood (Beverly Glen or San Vicente).
- **2:30 PM:** The Getty Center (1200 Getty Center Dr). Take the tram up to explore the collection, gardens, and architecture.
- **Evening:** Dinner in Brentwood or Santa Monica.

## Day 3 — Thursday, May 21 · Hollywood & Observatory
- **10:00 AM:** Hollywood Walk of Fame and TCL Chinese Theatre. 
- **1:00 PM:** Lunch on Larchmont Boulevard.
- **2:30 PM:** Academy Museum of Motion Pictures (6067 Wilshire Blvd).
- **6:30 PM:** Griffith Observatory (2800 E Observatory Rd). Arrive to see the Hollywood Sign in daylight, watch the sunset (~7:55 PM), and see the city lights.

## Day 4 — Friday, May 22 · Beverly Hills & West Hollywood
- **10:30 AM:** Rodeo Drive. Walk Canon Drive and Bedford Drive to experience the residential neighborhoods.
- **1:00 PM:** Lunch on Beverly Drive.
- **3:30 PM:** Sunset Strip, West Hollywood.
- **7:00 PM:** Dinner in West Hollywood (e.g., Gracias Madre or Craig's).

## Day 5 — Saturday, May 23 · PCH Drive → Santa Barbara
- **10:00 AM:** Pick up rental car in Santa Monica or West Hollywood (avoids LAX surcharge).
- **12:30 PM:** Lunch at Malibu Farm Pier Cafe (23000 Pacific Coast Hwy).
- **2:30 PM:** El Matador State Beach. Walk to the sea caves (check tide charts).
- **5:00 PM:** Hotel Californian check-in (36 State St, Santa Barbara).
- **Evening:** Walk to the Funk Zone for wine tasting and dinner.

## Day 6 — Sunday, May 24 · Santa Barbara
- **10:00 AM:** Old Mission Santa Barbara (2201 Laguna St). Arrive early to beat tour groups.
- **11:30 AM:** Brunch at Helena Avenue Bakery (131 Anacapa St).
- **Afternoon:** Relax along the Santa Barbara waterfront and State Street.
- **Evening:** Dinner near the hotel.

## Day 7 — Monday, May 25 · Return to LA & Downtown
- **10:00 AM:** Hotel Californian checkout. Drive 101 South via Ventura (~2 hours).
- **12:30 PM:** Lunch at Grand Central Market (317 S Broadway, Downtown LA).
- **2:30 PM:** The Broad (221 S Grand Ave — pre-book free timed ticket).
- **4:30 PM:** Check in to Downtown/Arts District hotel. Drop off the rental car.
- **Evening:** Uber to the Arts District. Dinner at Bestia or Bavel.

## Day 8 — Tuesday, May 26 · Departure
- **8:30 AM:** Breakfast at a local Arts District cafe (e.g., Bread Lounge).
- **10:00 AM:** Walk the Arts District to view local murals.
- **10:30 AM:** Uber directly to LAX. 
- **1:30 PM:** LY 6 departure — LAX → TLV.
