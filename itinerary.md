# Los Angeles & Santa Barbara — May 19–26, 2026

Two people, one week. Flying in from Tel Aviv (El Al). First visit to LA for both of us.

---

Four days in Los Angeles, a drive up the Pacific Coast Highway on Saturday, two nights in Santa Barbara, then back to LA for the final stretch. Car-free in the city (Uber), rental car only for the coast drive.

## Day 2 — Wednesday, May 20 · The Getty

The Getty Center is world-class. Half a day here is right — the collection, the gardens, the Pacific view from the terrace. Lunch in Brentwood afterward.

| Time | Stop | Address |
|------|------|---------|
| 10:30 AM | The Getty Center | 1200 Getty Center Dr, Brentwood (tram from Level 2 parking) |
| 1:00 PM | Lunch in Brentwood | Beverly Glen or San Vicente |
| Evening | Easy, Westside | — |

**Must do:** Take the tram up — the campus reveal sets the mood. Robert Irwin's Central Garden (the path curves so you can never see the whole thing). West-facing terrace for Pacific views.

---

## Day 3 — Thursday, May 21 · Academy Museum + Griffith Observatory

The Academy Museum is the best museum in LA right now. Griffith Observatory timed for an hour before sunset.

| Time | Stop | Address |
|------|------|---------|
| 10:00 AM | Academy Museum of Motion Pictures | 6067 Wilshire Blvd, Mid-Wilshire |
| 1:30 PM | Lunch on Larchmont Boulevard | Larchmont Village |
| 5:30 PM | Griffith Observatory | 2800 E Observatory Rd, Los Feliz |

**Must do:** The Sphere building at the Academy (the glass globe — most distinctive architecture built in LA in a decade). Arrive Griffith at 5:30 PM — 90 minutes of light left, the city turns from gold to purple. Walk to the east-facing platform.

## Practical Notes

**Weather (late May)**
- Los Angeles: 20–26°C, mostly sunny. "May Gray" marine layer in mornings is possible.
- Santa Barbara: 18–22°C, reliably sunny and clear.

**Getting around**
- Days 1–4: Uber everywhere. LA is very Uber-friendly.
- Day 5: Pick up rental car in Santa Monica or West Hollywood. Avoids the ~$30/day LAX airport surcharge.
- Days 5–7: Car needed for PCH drive, Santa Barbara, and return.
- Day 8: Uber to LAX or return the car there with time to spare.

**Packing**
- Layers: coastal evenings are cool, afternoons are warm.
- One nice outfit for dinner reservations.
- Walking shoes — State Street, Abbot Kinney, and the Arts District are all on foot.
